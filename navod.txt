z tohto linku https://sourceforge.net/projects/cunit-msvc/files/ si stiahni CUnit-msvc-2.1-3.exe
spusti inštalátor CUnit-msvc-2.1-3.exe
ulozi sa ti to pravdepodobne do C:\Program Files (x86)\CUnit, otvor si to

vo Visual Studiu v Solution Explorer klikni pravým na svoj projekt a vyber Properties
prejdi na C/C++ → General → Additional Include Directories
pridaj cestu k hlavičkovým súborom CUnit, pravdepodobne: C:\Program Files (x86)\CUnit\Include

v rovnakom okne prejdi na Linker → General → Additional Library Directories
pridaj cestu k knižniciam CUnit, napríklad: C:\Program Files (x86)\CUnit\Lib\Debug-x64

prejdi na Linker → Input → Additional Dependencies
pridaj CUnit.lib

buildni

ak chces spustit main: pravym klikni na test_project.c -> Properties -> General -> Excluded from Build -> Yes
spusti

ak chces spustit test_project (jednotkove testy): pravym klikni na test_project.c -> Properties -> General -> Excluded from Build -> No
pravym klikni na main.c -> Properties -> General -> Excluded from Build -> Yes
spusti
